# 🚚 Supply Chain Performance Analysis & Late Delivery Prediction System

An end-to-end Supply Chain Analytics project built using **Python, SQL, Power BI, DAX, and Machine Learning** to uncover operational bottlenecks, identify revenue risks, and predict late deliveries before shipment.

This project analyzes **172,765 supply chain transactions** and transforms raw operational data into actionable business insights through advanced analytics, interactive dashboards, stakeholder reports, and predictive machine learning models.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Modern supply chains generate enormous volumes of operational data, but without proper analysis, critical inefficiencies often remain hidden.

In this project, I performed a comprehensive supply chain analysis to identify:

- Causes of late deliveries
- Revenue exposure due to shipment delays
- Bottlenecks across regions, departments, and shipping modes
- Operational inefficiencies in logistics performance
- High-risk orders using Machine Learning prediction models

The project combines:
- Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Business Intelligence Dashboards
- KPI Reporting
- Predictive Machine Learning
- Executive-Level Stakeholder Reporting

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The company was facing severe operational inefficiencies including:

- High late delivery rates
- Revenue loss risks due to delayed shipments
- Poor logistics performance
- Inefficient shipping modes
- Regional bottlenecks
- Lack of predictive monitoring systems

The goal was to build a data-driven analytics solution capable of:
- Monitoring supply chain performance
- Detecting operational bottlenecks
- Identifying high-risk shipments
- Predicting late deliveries before they occur
- Supporting strategic business decisions

---

# 📊 Key Business Insights

## 🚨 Major Findings

### ⚠️ 54.71% Late Delivery Rate
More than half of all orders were delivered late, indicating a systemic logistics problem.

### 💰 $19.25M Revenue at Risk
Delayed shipments accounted for:
- **54.7% of total revenue**
- Significant customer attrition risk
- Potential refund & cancellation exposure

### 🚨 First Class Shipping Failure
A critical operational issue was discovered:

- **100% of First Class orders were delayed**
- **26,513 premium shipments affected**
- **$5.41M revenue exposed to operational failure**

### 🌍 Regional Bottleneck Detection
Regions with highest delay rates:
- Central Africa
- Southeast Asia
- Eastern Europe

### 🧠 Machine Learning Prediction System
A tuned **Random Forest Classifier** achieved:

- **86% Accuracy**
- **85% F1 Score**

allowing the business to proactively identify high-risk orders before delays occur.
